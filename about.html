About Bokku!

Your Trusted Source for Affordable Grocery Products in Nigeria

Get to Know Bokku!

Bokku! is a discount grocery store chain that started in Nigeria on September 30, 2022, inspired by a similar model from Germany. We provide quality grocery solutions, delivering affordable and reliable products to meet the needs of Nigerian households.

Bokku! has become a trusted name in Nigeria’s grocery market, offering high-quality products that meet 80% of the average household’s daily needs. With a carefully selected range that includes staple foods, personal care items, and household essentials, we ensure shoppers get the best products from trusted suppliers.
